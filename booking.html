About

The Handles are a heavy alternative rock band with a dark, atmospheric edge.
Their live shows are high-energy and crowd-focused, built to engage audiences and keep rooms moving.
Since forming in 2023, the band have built a strong local following through a reputation for delivering energetic, engaging sets.
The band have performed at venues including O2 Academy Islington, Dingwalls, and 93 Feet East.
