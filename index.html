The Handles

The Handles are a five-piece rock band from Chelmsford, Essex, blending raw energy, tight riffs, and honest lyrics that get crowds moving. Made up of Mat (vocals, rhythm), Thom and Max (guitars), Ben (bass), and Logan (drums), they’ve built a name through loud, chaotic, and unforgettable live shows. Their debut EP Witch Hunt captures themes of rebellion and release — a call to break free, turn it up, and lose yourself in the noise. No gimmicks, just five mates making music that actually means something.
